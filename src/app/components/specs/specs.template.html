How does it work?

Place your PetBot somewhere your pet can access, and fill it with their favorite treats.

Quick-response motion sensors will recognize your pet’s adorable face and automatically dispense a treat.

Meanwhile the high-resolution camera records the cutest selfie of your pet getting super excited.

You check your notifications, say “awwww”, and post the selfie to social media.
